About Us

Guangzhou Honvally Electronic Technology

Your Trusted LED Lighting Partner Since 2017

Located in Guangzhou, China, Honvally is a professional LED lighting manufacturer specializing in COB track lights, recessed downlights, magnetic track systems, and outdoor lighting solutions.

We partner with world-leading component brands including Philips, Lifud, Bridgelux, CREE, and Osram to ensure every product meets the highest standards of performance, efficiency, and longevity.

With a 100,000-unit monthly production capacity and dedicated R&D team, we offer OEM/ODM services and customized lighting solutions for projects of any scale.
